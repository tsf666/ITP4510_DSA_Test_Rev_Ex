## 📄 Open Learning & Collaborative Growth | 开放学习与共同成长

### 💡 Philosophy | 初心与理念

I believe that everything happens for a reason. Every experience, including the accidental exposure of my assignments due to system vulnerabilities, is a necessary step in our personal growth. Rather than viewing this as a privacy breach, I see it as an opportunity for collective progress.

我始终相信万事皆有因缘。每个人所经历的一切——包括因教务系统漏洞而导致的作业公开——都是成长的必经之路。与其将其视为隐私泄露，我更倾向于将其看作是一次共同进步的契机。

In the ivory tower, we are all "practitioners" seeking knowledge. Without the conflict of interest found in the secular world, we should support and learn from one another.

校园之内，众生皆为修道人。既然没有世俗社会的利益冲突，我们理应互相成就，砥砺前行。

***

### 🚀 To My Peers | 致同行者

If you find my work helpful for your studies, you are more than welcome to explore it here. This repository is created to move from "passive exposure" to **active sharing and exchange.**

如果你认为我的功课对你的学习有所裨益，欢迎在此参考。建立这个仓库的初衷，是将“被动公开”转化为**主动的分享与交流。**

* **Open Exchange:** I encourage discussions, suggestions, and collaborative learning.

* **开放交流：** 欢迎任何形式的讨论、建议与经验交换。

***

### ⚖️ The Principle of Reciprocity | 关于价值交换

While I advocate for sharing, please understand that **generosity is a choice, not an obligation.** 虽然我提倡分享，但也请理解：**慷慨是一种选择，而非义务。**

I may not fulfill every request for my assignments. My willingness to share depends on whether I perceive qualities worthy of my generosity or the potential for a meaningful exchange of value. True learning is a two-way street; I look for peers who can bring their own unique insights to the table.

对于索取作业的请求，我有时可能会拒绝。这取决于我是否在对方身上看到值得我慷慨解囊的特质，或是否存在值得交换的价值。真正的学习是双向的，我期待遇到能带着独特见解而来的同行者。

***

### 🤝 How to Connect | 如何联系

Feel free to open an **Issue** or submit a **Pull Request** if you have insights to share. Let us grow together through this shared journey.

若有任何心得，欢迎通过 **Issue** 或 **Pull Request** 与我沟通。愿我们在这段共同的旅程中各有所获。
